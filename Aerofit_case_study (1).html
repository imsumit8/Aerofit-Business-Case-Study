Summarized Recommendations¶

Customer Profiling : KP281¶

  1. Gender: Females are more likely to buy this product comaped to male counterparts.
  2. Income: Customers in the income range of 30k-50k are more likely to buy KP-281.

  3. Age: Customers in the age group of 18-30 are more likely to buy KP281.

  4. Other considerations: Customers with fitness level 1-3 are more likely to buy this product.

Customer Profiling : KP481¶

  1. Gender: Females are more likely to buy this product compared to male counterparts.
  2. Income: Customers in the income range of 50k-70k are more likely to buy KP-481.
  3. Age: Customers in the age group of 30-40 are more likely to buy this product.

Customer Profiling : KP781¶

  1. Gender: Males are more likely to buy this product comaped to female counterpart.
  2. Income: Customers in the income range of 70k+ are most likely to buy KP-781.

  3. Age: The age of a person doesn't really matter for this product. If someone is wealthy or very fit, they're likely to purchase it.

  4. Other considerations: Customers with fitness level 4-5 or average 140 miles per week or average usage of more than 4 days per week are more likely to buy this product.
